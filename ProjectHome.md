This little tool will automatically translate Rails localization yaml data into your specific languages.

e.g.

[Rails local yaml](Source.md)
en:
> global:
> > click\_here:   "Click here"
> > submit:       "Submit"
> > cancel:       "Cancel"
> > preview:      "Preview"
> > create:       "Create"
> > edit:         "Edit"
